W How to Use
🎯 Your Tiles

Type up to 9 letters in the tile input. Use ? for blank tiles. Your tiles appear as visual letter tiles above the input. Tap Find Words to search.

📋 Game Log

Track every turn of your game. Tap + My Play or + Opp to log a word, the tiles placed, and the score.

  • Bag counter — shows how many of each tile remain in the bag.
  • Danger tiles — estimates the chance your opponent holds high-value tiles like Q, Z, J, X, or blanks.
  • Tap ✕ on any logged turn to delete it.
🔡 Pattern Mode

Model specific board positions before searching.

  • Tap + Add to create a pattern slot.
  • Each slot shows a row of cells — tap any cell to set the fixed letter already on the board.
  • Leave cells empty (?) for positions you'll fill with your tiles.
  • Use − and + to shrink or grow the pattern length.
  • Results are grouped by pattern, then show all other possible words.
🟦 Board Mode

Enter the full board state for the most accurate plays.

  • Tap a cell to select it (highlighted cursor), then type letters on your keyboard or phone.
  • Tap the same cell again to toggle typing direction between → Right and ↓ Down.
  • Use the → Right / ↓ Down button to switch direction without re-tapping.
  • Pinch to zoom the board for easier navigation.
  • ↩ Undo reverses your last board edit. Clear resets the board.
  • Save up to 5 game slots — switching slots auto-saves the current board.
📖 Word Results
  • Tap any word to open a drawer showing the definition, score breakdown, and (in Board Mode) a mini board preview.
  • Long press a word on the board grid to see its definition and delete it.
  • In Board Mode, tap ✓ Apply play to board to place the word automatically.
  • Scores include letter multipliers (DL/TL) and word multipliers (DW/TW).
  • BINGO ⭐ means all 7 tiles were used — worth a +35 bonus.
  • The leave badge (🟢🟡🔴) rates how good your remaining tiles are after playing.
🔍 Filters & Sorting
  • Use the Min Score slider to hide low-scoring words.
  • Add words to Exclude to prevent them from appearing (e.g. words already played).
  • Sort results by Score, Length, or A–Z.
🕐 Search History

Tap the 🕐 icon in the header to see your recent tile searches. Tap any history entry to re-run that search instantly.
